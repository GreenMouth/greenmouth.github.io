about

hi, i'm prashant

i'm a data scientist by craft and a drone pilot by curiosity. flying gives me a feel for the places i'm in — the shape of a coastline, the grid of a city, the quiet of a forest from a few hundred feet up.

i grew up in india and now call the u.s. home — a mix of two worlds i carry with me. away from the screen and the sticks, i play cricket for the atlanta chargers in leagues like super bash and the atlanta cricket league, and i've loved football since i played for my school team. i make music in logic pro, edit video in final cut, and i'll take a white-sand beach over a mountain any day.

i meditate, practice breathwork, and spend a lot of my inner life exploring spirituality and human consciousness. travel and time in nature are how i refill. there's more to the story — best told over a cup of chai.

the other half of me

by day, i'm a data scientist.

for 8+ years i've built machine learning at companies like carter's, walmart, and accenture — work i genuinely love, that taught me patience, precision, and how to spot a pattern in the noise.

flying is how i get out of the screen. it scratches the same itch from the opposite direction: instead of chasing signal in data, i'm chasing light, weather, and the perfect frame. two different ways of paying close attention to the world — and i bring the same care to both.
