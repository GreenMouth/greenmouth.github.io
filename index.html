the other half of me

by day, i'm a data scientist.

8+ years building machine learning at carter's, walmart, and accenture — work that taught me patience, precision, and how to find the signal in the noise.

it began with a master's at the university of texas at dallas (2015–2017), and a second at georgia tech i left before finishing.

flying is how i step away from the screen — same instinct, opposite direction. instead of signal in data, i'm chasing light, weather, and the perfect frame.
